# Report Blueprint

## Page 1: Executive Overview

Purpose: Give leaders a fast read on total performance and where to investigate.

Recommended visuals:

- KPI cards: Net Sales, Gross Profit, Gross Margin %, Total Orders, Return Rate %
- Line chart: Net Sales by Month
- Clustered column chart: Net Sales by Region
- Bar chart: Net Sales by Product Category
- Matrix: Region by Category with Net Sales, Gross Profit, Gross Margin %
- Slicers: Year, Region, Sales Channel, Customer Segment

Insights to call out:

- Best performing region and category
- YoY revenue movement
- Whether margin is improving or weakening

## Page 2: Product and Margin Analysis

Purpose: Explain what is selling and whether sales quality is healthy.

Recommended visuals:

- Scatter chart: Net Sales vs Gross Margin % by Product
- Bar chart: Top 10 Products by Net Sales
- Bar chart: Discount Rate by Category
- Matrix: Category, Subcategory, Product with Net Sales, Gross Profit, Discount Rate
- KPI card: Top Category By Net Sales

Insights to call out:

- Products with high revenue but low margin
- Categories relying heavily on discounts
- Products that can support premium placement

## Page 3: Customer and Channel Insights

Purpose: Show customer value and channel behavior.

Recommended visuals:

- Donut chart: Net Sales by Customer Segment
- Column chart: Orders Per Customer by Segment
- Bar chart: Net Sales by Acquisition Channel
- Line chart: Net Sales by Sales Channel over Month
- Matrix: Segment by Age Band with Sales Per Customer and Total Orders

Insights to call out:

- Whether loyal customers are materially more valuable
- Which acquisition channels are generating higher-value customers
- Online versus store performance trends

## Page 4: Returns and Inventory Watchlist

Purpose: Identify operational drag from returns and stock pressure.

Recommended visuals:

- KPI cards: Return Amount, Return Rate %, Returned Units, Products Below Reorder Point
- Bar chart: Return Amount by Return Reason
- Bar chart: Return Rate % by Category
- Table: Products below reorder point with store, product, on-hand, reorder point, units to target
- Matrix: Store by Category inventory units on hand

Insights to call out:

- Categories with the highest return risk
- Return reasons that suggest product or fulfillment issues
- Stores and products requiring replenishment

## Design Guidance

- Use a muted professional theme with teal as the main accent and amber for warnings.
- Put slicers in a consistent left rail or top band.
- Keep KPI cards in the same order on every page.
- Use dynamic titles that include `Selected Period Label`.
- Favor business labels over field names, for example `Gross Margin %` instead of `Gross_Margin_Pct`.
